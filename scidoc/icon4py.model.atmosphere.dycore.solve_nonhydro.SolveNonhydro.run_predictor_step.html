    \[ \exnhydrocorr{\e} = \frac{g}{\cpd} \Wedge 4 \frac{ \vpotemp{}{\c_1}{\k} - \vpotemp{}{\c_0}{\k} }{ (\vpotemp{}{\c_1}{\k} + \vpotemp{}{\c_0}{\k})^2 }, \]
    with
    \[ \vpotemp{}{\c_i}{\k} = \vpotemp{}{\c_i}{\k^*} + \dzgradp \frac{\vpotemp{}{\c_i}{\k^*-1/2} - \vpotemp{}{\c_i}{\k^*+1/2}}{\Dz{\k^*}} \]
    Compute the hydrostatically approximated correction term that replaces the downward extrapolation (last term in eq. 10 in Zangl_2012). This is only computed for the bottom-most level because all edges which have a neighboring cell center inside terrain beyond a certain limit use the same correction term at \(k^*\) level in eq. 10 in Zangl_2012 (see also the last paragraph on page 3724 for the discussion). \(\c_i\) are the indexes of the adjacent cell centers using \(\offProv{e2c}\); \(k^*\) is the level index of the neighboring (horizontally, not terrain-following) cell center and \(h^*\) is its height.
